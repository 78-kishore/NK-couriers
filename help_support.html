Help & Support

If you have any questions or need assistance, our support team is here to help. Below you can find answers to some common questions and ways to get in touch with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided at the time of dispatch. Simply Contact 7868091722

What are your delivery times?

Our delivery times vary based on the service selected. Local deliveries typically take 1-2 days, national deliveries 3-5 days, and international deliveries 5-10 days. For more specific information, please refer to our Services page.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed, please contact our customer support team at 9361026611 or email us at nkcouriers@gmsil.com. We will investigate the issue and provide you with an update as soon as possible.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via phone at 9361026611 or email at nkcouriers@gmsil.com. We are available Monday to Friday, 7 AM to 9 PM.
